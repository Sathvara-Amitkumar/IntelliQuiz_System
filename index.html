How IntelliQuiz Works

From document upload to student assessment in three simple steps

01

Upload & Generate

Upload your study materials or course documents. Our AI analyzes the content and generates relevant, intelligent questions automatically.

  • Support for PDF, DOCX, TXT formats
  • Custom AI prompts for question styles
  • Multiple difficulty levels
02

Review, edit, and customize generated questions. Add students, assign faculty, and set quiz parameters from your admin dashboard.
